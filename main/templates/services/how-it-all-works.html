THE PROCESS

What I offer is a path to self discovery and fulfillment by leading you through a process that explores what it is you believe in and what you want to contribute to the outer world. My mission is to provide a safe and powerful place for you to discover the answers you need in order to move forward with purpose and clarity. With structure comes freedom. For each of the foundational sessions and courses, there will be an area of focus coupled with assessments, prep work and take away actions.

THE FIVE STEP PROCESS
Session One - Your Story

You will begin the coaching process by stating your goal for the session. It is a chance for you to See all the possibilities that your future holds. Imagine the satisfaction and pleasure as you reach your goal! Setting a measurable, achievable, timely goal is the key here. Enthusiasm, passion and energy around your goal are elements that will help support and sustain your efforts towards achieving your goals.

Session Two - Assessing Your Current Reality

The next part of the process will focus on working to uncover what is holding you from reaching your full potential. Through a number of different tools, I will partner with you to help you gain clarity of purpose. Uncovering and identifying your purpose will provide you with an internal set of guidelines that you can use consistently to test your choices against, making sure that your decisions align with who you truly are. When you own your unique set of purposeful skills and talents, you can then paint the picture to new people and current leadership about who you are and what you are ready for in your career

Session Three - Options

Through this step you will map your route to your goal. Now that the destination is established, how will you get there? We examine what support and resources you will need to get there

Session Four - Action Planning

After the options are complete, comes the action planning. I will help you to create short and long term actions in all areas of your life. This action plan will feel INSPIRING, connected to WHAT YOU WANT in your life and DO-ABLE. How will you reach your goal? Capture and document your plan and keep it visible to help you stay focused on your end in mind.

Session Five - Outcome

How will you feel when you achieve your goal? Imagine the feeling. As your coach, I will hold you responsible for completing the actions you commit to over the following months. You will report on your progress and we will create a long-term action plan for moving forward.

So now that you know the journey to long-term change...
