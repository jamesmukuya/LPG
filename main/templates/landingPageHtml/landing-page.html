MEET SUSAN

I applaud you for choosing this path of your self- improvement process and for putting yourself on the path to living a fulfilling life on all levels. I want the same for you. I am a stand for you to be who you truly are, do what you love and have what you deserve. You are here to leave a legacy and make an impact on the world. You are here for a purpose. Making a difference begins with you.

As your partner and coach, I will support you in identifying, acknowledging and getting rid of the blocks to your purpose. I will challenge you to say “no” to going through the motions of life and enthusiastically say “yes” to the overflow that is available to you. We begin by taking an overview of your life and identifying areas that need action and/or attention. I will assist you in establishing clear, concrete, measurable goals and support you in connecting to your own inner wisdom. There is nothing you cannot shift. You are capable of so much more than you know or are currently creating.

Whether you have a specific goal or are seeking general guidance, our relationship will evolve in a way that best supports you. I will hold you accountable to a plan that is manageable and created especially for you. I do not solve your problems, but guide you into solving them on your own because the will power is within you somewhere. Your every action will become more intentional. I listen with understanding and 100% confidentiality. My relationships with my clients are intimate, honest, and rewarding.

I am a Certified Life Coach (CLC) but my most important and extensive training comes from my life experiences and gifts. Getting to the other side of career confusion and business failure, debt, family break-downs, relationship issues and endings, have taught me life lessons that I am inspired to teach you. I believe we are all our own best inner counsellor and coach, but sometimes we simply need someone else to help get us started. Coaching sessions can be conducted via phone, Skype or in person. A minimum three months commitment is required up front because that gives you time to truly create change in your life. I also offer one-day intensive breakthrough sessions for people seeking immediate clarity.

My clients are very diverse in terms of demographics. I work with men and women of all ages who are CEO’s, entrepreneurs, parents, managers, students, thought-leaders, and so on. The one thing they all have in common is a yearning for more aligned success, connection, fulfillment, and impact. I only work with four clients at a time who are committed to being held accountable, showing up 100% for each session, and are willing to be break out of their comfort zone. Wishing you many blessings on your journey and I acknowledge you for knowing and seeing your true potential. I look forward to working with you or recommending another coach that is a fit. I am here to support you!
